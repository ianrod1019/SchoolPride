About Booker T Washington High School

Booker T Washington High School is a Tulsa Public School that is located in Tulsa, Oklahoma.

  • Booker T Washington mascot is the Hornet.

  • Booker T Washington's colors are Orange and Black. They are meaningful because they represent the school's spirit and pride.

  • Booker T Washington's mission is "We prepare students to shape a better world with courage, compassion, and purpose." - BTW TPS webpage

  • I am in 10th grade and I have attended Booker T Washington High School for 1 year.

  • My favorite thing about Booker T Washington High School is the student body, as it very diverse and inclusive.

  • Some special traditions that we have in Booker T Washington High School include:

    • The School Hymn: After every sport game we sing the school Hymn, and at the beginning of every assembly.
    • Homecoming Week: A week full of activities, including a parade, spirit days, and a football game.
  • Booker T Washington High School is very unique because it has a rich history and a strong sense of community. It is known for its academic excellence, diverse student body, and commitment to preparing students for success in life. On top of that, Hornet pride runs deep in the school, with students and alumni showing their support through various events and activities.
